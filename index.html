B. Pharm Lateral

"B.Pharm lateral entry" or "B.Pharm lateral course" typically refers to a program designed for students who have completed a Diploma in Pharmacy (D.Pharm) or an equivalent qualification. These programs allow students to enter directly into the second or third year of the Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm) program, depending on the structure of the curriculum.
